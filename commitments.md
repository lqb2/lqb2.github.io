> Your Yes Means Nothing if You Can’t Say No — Dr. Neha Sangwan

Like many of us, I get asked to do lots of things (join this board, read this piece, comment on this article, attend this event, go to this place, take on this work). Also like many of us, I struggle with saying no. The struggle is especially intense when the ask is for something I *really* want to do. However, I have learned that every no I say is energy/time/focus that I get to keep pouring into things I've already committed to and every yes, in some way, takes away from my commitments. And yet, the struggle persists. So I put together this list of commitments to help myself (and others) understand what I'm already holding so that I/we can think intentionally about what happens when I say yes.
